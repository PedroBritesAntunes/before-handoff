How Before Handoff works

Before Handoff helps Product Designers spot gaps and risks before sending work to engineering. Fewer back-and-forths, fewer surprises in dev.

  1. Pick what you're handing off — component, screen or flow.
  2. Add interface type and product context.
  3. Describe the behavior — be specific about interactions, edge cases and rules.
  4. Tick the states you've already considered.
  5. Click Run risk check. The OpenAI model generates a tailored checklist with severity and readiness score.
  6. Copy the report into your ticket or design doc.

This is an alpha — feedback welcome.
